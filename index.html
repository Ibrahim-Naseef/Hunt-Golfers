ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TOP RACER

Speed through the tracks and emerge victorious as the top racer in this thrilling competition. Navigate challenging turns, avoid obstacles, and feel the adrenaline rush with every acceleration. Are you ready for the ultimate racing experience?

ADVENTURE GOLF

Embark on a journey of excitement and skill as you navigate the twists and turns of our adventure golf course. Immerse yourself in a world of fun challenges, tricky holes, and surprising obstacles. Discover the joy of adventure golf like never before!

GOLF LESSONS

Enhance your golfing skills with our expert-led lessons. Whether you're a beginner looking to grasp the basics or an experienced golfer aiming to refine your technique, our lessons cater to all levels. Join us on the green and take your golfing journey to new heights!
